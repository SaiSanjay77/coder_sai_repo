Our Programs

  • B.Tech in Computer Science: Offers syllabus in computer science with various programming languages such as C, C++, Python, Java, JavaScript, Git, Node, React, Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems, DBMS, Computer Networks, Web Development, and Software Engineering.
  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication: Covers Analog and Digital Electronics, Microprocessors, Communication Systems, Signal Processing, VLSI Design, Embedded Systems, Antenna Theory, Wireless Communication, and IoT.
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering: Includes Engineering Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Manufacturing Processes, Machine Design, Heat Transfer, CAD/CAM, Robotics, and Automobile Engineering.
  • M.Tech in Data Science: Focuses on Data Mining, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Big Data Analytics, Data Visualization, Statistical Methods, Python/R Programming, and Cloud Computing for Data Science.
  • M.Tech in Software Engineering: Covers Software Development Life Cycle, Agile Methodologies, Advanced Algorithms, Software Architecture, Testing and Quality Assurance, DevOps, and Project Management.
  • M.Tech in Cybersecurity: Includes Network Security, Cryptography, Ethical Hacking, Digital Forensics, Security Protocols, Cloud Security, and Cyber Law.
  • M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence: Focuses on AI Fundamentals, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Robotics, and AI Ethics.
  • M.Tech in Cloud Computing: Covers Cloud Architecture, Virtualization, Distributed Systems, Cloud Security, AWS/Azure/GCP Platforms, DevOps, and Cloud Application Development.
  • M.Tech in Embedded Systems: Includes Embedded C, Microcontrollers, RTOS, IoT, VLSI Design, Hardware-Software Co-Design, and System Integration.
  • M.Tech in Power Electronics: Covers Power Semiconductor Devices, Converters, Inverters, Motor Drives, Renewable Energy Systems, and Control of Power Electronic Systems.
  • M.Tech in Communication Systems: Focuses on Digital and Analog Communication, Wireless Networks, Optical Communication, Satellite Communication, and Signal Processing.
  • M.Tech in Structural Engineering: Includes Structural Analysis, Concrete and Steel Structures, Earthquake Engineering, Construction Materials, and Finite Element Methods.
  • M.Tech in Thermal Engineering: Covers Thermodynamics, Heat and Mass Transfer, IC Engines, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning, Power Plant Engineering, and Computational Fluid Dynamics.
  • M.Tech in Design Engineering: Focuses on Advanced Machine Design, CAD/CAM, Finite Element Analysis, Product Design, Materials Engineering, and Industrial Design.
  • M.Tech in Robotics: Includes Robotics Fundamentals, Kinematics and Dynamics, Control Systems, Sensors and Actuators, AI for Robotics, and Industrial Automation.
  • M.Tech in Data Science: Focuses on Data Mining, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Big Data Analytics, Data Visualization, Statistical Methods, Python/R Programming, and Cloud Computing for Data Science.
  • M.Tech in VLSI Design: Covers Digital and Analog VLSI Design, CMOS Technology, FPGA, ASIC Design, HDL Programming, and Semiconductor Devices.
